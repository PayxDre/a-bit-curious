Quick-reference facts

  • Supply cap: 21,000,000 BTC (actually 20,999,999.9769)
  • Smallest unit: 1 satoshi = 0.00000001 BTC
  • Genesis block: January 3, 2009
  • Creator: Satoshi Nakamoto (pseudonymous, unknown identity)
  • Consensus: Proof of work (SHA-256)
  • Block time: ~10 minutes (adjusted every 2,016 blocks)
  • Block reward: 3.125 BTC (since April 2024 halving)
  • Next halving: ~2028
  • Network uptime: 99.99%+ since January 2009
  • Hash rate: 700+ EH/s
  • Full nodes: 50,000+
  • Lightning capacity: 5,600+ BTC, 15,000+ nodes
  • Energy usage: ~150 TWh/year (vs ~260 TWh/year traditional finance)
  • Illicit activity: less than 0.5% of crypto volume (Chainalysis)
  • USD purchasing power lost since 1913: ~97%
  • Bitcoin market cap vs global assets: ~0.19% of ~$900T
